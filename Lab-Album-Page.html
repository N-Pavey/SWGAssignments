How Big, How Blue, How Beautiful

Florence + the Machine

2015

This album is the band's third studio album. While sticking to their Indie Rock roots, this album features less instrument-heavy songs with an emphasis on folk, blues, and gospel tones. How Big, How Blue, How Beautiful has won 5 Grammy Award nominations and has released 4 singles: "What Kind of Man," "Ship to Wreck," "Queen of Peace," and "Delilah."

Band Members

Florence Welch - Song Writer/Lead Vocalist

Chris Hayden - Drums and Percussion
